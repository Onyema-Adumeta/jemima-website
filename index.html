About Jemima

Jemima Adumeta is a young storyteller who loves creating kind, meaningful stories. Chloe’s Best Friend is her first published book, inspired by her mom, dad, and big brother.

“Keep reading, keep writing, and most of all—keep being YOU.” 💖

— Jemima

The Book

Chloe’s Best Friend follows Chloe and Sam as they learn what it means to love, to miss someone, and to stay brave through big changes. It’s a gentle reminder that friendship can carry us through hard moments — and that new beginnings can still bring joy.
