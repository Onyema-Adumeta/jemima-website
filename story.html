Chloe’s Best Friend

By Jemima Adumeta

Chloe was a cheerful little girl who loved to laugh, play, and explore. Every day felt like an adventure to her.

One sunny afternoon, Chloe went to the park with her favourite toy and a big smile. She ran across the grass, watched the birds, and enjoyed the warm breeze.

While she was playing, she noticed another child sitting alone on a bench. The girl looked quiet and a little shy.

Chloe walked over gently and said, “Hi, my name is Chloe. Do you want to play with me?”

The girl looked up and smiled. “My name is Sam,” she said.

Soon, Chloe and Sam were laughing together, playing games, and sharing happy stories. They climbed, ran, and imagined they were explorers in a magical land.

By the end of the day, Chloe knew something special had happened. She had made a new best friend.

From that day on, Chloe and Sam played together often. They shared kindness, fun, and friendship wherever they went.

Chloe learned that sometimes the best friendships begin with a simple hello.
