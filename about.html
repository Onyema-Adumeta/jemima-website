About Jemima

Young writer, big imagination, and a heart full of stories about friendship, kindness, and growing up.

Jemima in her element 🍂🎾

Children's Author

Jemima Adumeta

Jemima is a young, passionate storyteller with a gift for writing stories that speak directly to children's hearts. Growing up, she found that the best friendships are the ones that are tested — and come out stronger on the other side.

Her debut book, Chloe's Best Friend, was born from that belief. It's a story about two girls navigating the bumps that every real friendship goes through — told with warmth, honesty, and hope.

When she's not writing, Jemima loves being outdoors, staying active, and dreaming up her next story. She believes every child deserves to see themselves in the pages of a book.
